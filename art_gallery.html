Artwork 1: Starry Night Over the Ocean

This piece, completed in 2023, captures the raw energy of a cosmic event mirrored on the sea's surface. The artist used a palette knife technique but later chose bold brushstrokes for the final texture.
The inspiration came from long exposures of celestial phenomena.
